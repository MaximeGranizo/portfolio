My civic engagement recap


The Climate Fresk


The climate fresk is a workshop which made us ask various questions on important social and environmental issues.
The goal is to understand how causes and consequences of climate change are linked together. They are represented by cards that we have arranged together by respecting the chronology of events.

The Sulitest


It is a test subdivided on topics such as transition towards sustainability or global and local human-constructed systems.
It aims to evaluate our knowledge on sustainability literacy to increase our awareness of sustainable development. It is essential to take informed decisions in line with the respect of the environment.
It taught me that there are domains where I should to learn about and enables me to have a wider vision on the subject.

Theatre piece
"the King-Kong's trial"


I participated to an interactive play that invited us to take place in a court. Indeed, the judge of this somewhat special institution, called on the public to help him understand and judge. We were therefore faced with three cases in which the culprits take themselves for the victims, but more seriously, the victims think they are guilty. Axel, Alex and Omar had three crazy integration nights at their new school. In addition to the different hazing that they would have liked to avoid, each in turn found themselves in a position of having a harassing, sexist or racist behavior. But none of them can see when they were guilty, victims, accomplices or simply innocent.This extraordinary court has an advantage, the protagonists play live the various incriminated moments. And the public, through a moving debate, can clearly position itself in space and change its position as the exchanges and arguments are shared.

It was interesting because it made us aks to ourselves important questions on subject that could happen to us.

Cordées de la réussite


I am animating with other engineer students and professors, volunteer workshop which aims to provide support to younger students in their orientation process. The objective is to introduce greater social equity in access to higher education.

Shift project conference


I assisted to a conference about climate change organized by the shift project.
The Shift Project is a French think tank advocating the shift to a post-carbon economy. As a non-profit organization committed to serving the general interest through scientific objectivity, we are dedicated to informing and influencing the debate on energy transition in Europe.

Creathlon Day team project


In group of five, we found a startup idea and we worked on it for one day, to develop it and create a 3 minutes pitch which aims to present our idea to our classmates and finally elect the best idea. We were helped by few trained entrepreneurs who have already created their own businesses.
